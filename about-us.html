Brenda Jow

Meet Brenda Jow! Who said "Thanks to my dad who made up his last name so no one actually knows how to pronounce my last name. My last name is pronounced “Jo”" She is orginally from Taiwan and moved to the U.S when she was 14 years old.

Brenda graduated from the University of Washington, majoring in Early Childhood and Families Studies. She found her first passion - teaching, when volunteering at a Chinese school, and ever since she's been working in the education field for almost 9 years. Now she found her second passion - coding.
